Curiosity

2012 - Present

Currently active and exploring Mars. Curiosity has been operational for over a decade, providing continuous data and imagery from the Martian surface.

  • Landing Date: August 6, 2012
  • Mission Status: Active
  • Location: Gale Crater

Perseverance

2021 - Present

The newest rover on Mars, equipped with advanced instruments and the Ingenuity helicopter.

  • Landing Date: February 18, 2021
  • Mission Status: Active
  • Location: Jezero Crater

Opportunity

2004 - 2018

Operated for 14 years, far exceeding its planned 90-day mission. Opportunity made numerous discoveries about Mars' geology and past water activity.

  • Landing Date: January 25, 2004
  • Mission End: June 10, 2018
  • Location: Meridiani Planum

Spirit

2004 - 2010

Twin rover to Opportunity, Spirit explored the Martian surface for nearly 7 years, helping scientists understand Mars' past climate.

  • Landing Date: January 4, 2004
  • Mission End: March 22, 2010
  • Location: Gusev Crater
